Our Mission

Standing with Israel in times of crisis, providing essential humanitarian aid and defense support to those who need it most.

Our Story

YasharProject was born from the urgent recognition that in times of crisis, immediate action saves lives. Founded by a coalition of concerned individuals from around the world, our organization emerged as a direct response to the escalating humanitarian needs in Israel during periods of intense conflict.

The name "Yashar" means "straight" or "direct" in Hebrew, reflecting our commitment to transparent, immediate aid delivery without bureaucratic delays. Every dollar donated goes directly to verified humanitarian and defense initiatives, with full accountability and regular reporting to our supporters.

"In moments of crisis, the difference between hope and despair often comes down to knowing that someone, somewhere, cares enough to act. YasharProject is that bridge between caring and action."

- Sarah Cohen, Founding Director

Since our inception, we have channeled over $2.3 million in emergency aid, supporting everything from bulletproof vests for first responders to emergency medical supplies for hospitals treating civilian casualties. Our network spans across continents, with supporters from North America, Europe, Australia, and beyond.

Yashar LaChayal stocks special supply closets on training bases throughout Israel. These supply closets provide soldiers in need with important necessities such as warm socks, toothbrushes, and deodorant.
